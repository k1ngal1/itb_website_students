About us


The Institut Tecnologic de Barcelona is a renowned technology institute that has been providing cutting-edge education in the field of technology for over two decades. Our institution is committed to nurturing the next generation of technology leaders, who will drive innovation and change in the world.

At the heart of our institute is our student body, which is composed of some of the brightest and most talented individuals in the field of technology. Our students come from diverse backgrounds and bring with them a range of experiences and perspectives, which enriches the learning environment and fosters a sense of community.

We are proud of our students' achievements, both inside and outside of the classroom. Our graduates have gone on to pursue successful careers in a range of fields, from software development and data analytics to cybersecurity and artificial intelligence. They are leaders and innovators, shaping the future of technology and making a positive impact on the world.

Our commitment to our students goes beyond the classroom. We offer a range of support services to help our students succeed, including academic advising, career counseling, and networking opportunities. We also provide a range of extracurricular activities and clubs, which allow students to pursue their interests and develop new skills.

At the Institut Tecnologic de Barcelona, we believe that technology has the power to change the world for the better. We are dedicated to providing our students with the education and skills they need to be part of this change, and to make a positive impact on society.
